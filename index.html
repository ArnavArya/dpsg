More About DPS GAYA

Martin Luther King Jr. said, 'The function of education is to teach one to think intensively and to think critically. Intelligence plus character - that is the goal of true education.' Keeping with the same ideology, we at Delhi Public School, Gaya endeavour to impart education, a combination of the Indian values and the western concept of a pragmatic approach to knowledge acquisition, by involving students in hand-on experience. Apart from academics, co-curricular activities are given equal prominence in the school. The National Olympiads are a regular feature of the school. Delhi Public School, Gaya is a place which prides itself in being a unique model of all-inclusive learning that suits best for the students who are passionately driven, inquisitive and self-sufficient. Delhi Public School, Gaya is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which is the largest educational board in the country. It is recognized by the Department of Education, Govt. of NCT Delhi and the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India. Delhi Public School, Gaya offers a nationalistic curriculum in an environment that fosters excellence in school life amidst wide open spaces. It also promotes the natural ability of children to learn through development of their cognitive and thinking skills.
